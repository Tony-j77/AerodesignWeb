Recruitment FAQ

What type of experience should I expect?

• Gain designing, prototyping, testing, and building experience
• Gain teamwork, communication, leadership, and project management skills
• Make new friends and industry connections
• Gain student team experience that is often mandatory for Co-op recruiting companies

What could I learn?

• The Design Process and fundamental theories of flight (i.e. Ground School)
• CAD with SolidWorks
• Materials and construction knowledge
• Analytical flow simulation using programs like MATLAB, ANSYS, and XFLR5​
• Electrical power systems knowledge

What we look for

• Friendly attitude and a teamwork mentality
• Interest in airplanes and the aerospace industry
• Outstanding long term commitment to the team
• Ability to work effectively, meet deadlines, and willingness to step
