UBC AERO DESIGN

We are an engineering student team at the University of British Columbia. The team designs, builds, and flies payload-lifting, radio-controlled fixed-wing airplanes to compete in the international SAE Aero Design Collegiate Series.​​

Learn More

The goal of regular class is simple: to build the most capable fixed wing aircraft that is reliable and can carry heavy payloads into the air. Snorlax is UBC Aero Design's regular class aircraft for 2021-2022 SAE Aero Design competition.

The advanced class goal is to build an electric-powered fixed-wing aircraft capable of providing an live video stream and dropping a payload onto a ground target. Kyogre is UBC Aero Design's advanced aircraft for the 2021-2022 SAE Aero Design competition.
